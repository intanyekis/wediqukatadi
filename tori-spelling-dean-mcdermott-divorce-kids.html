For a while now, there have also been some news stories that mention Tori Spelling's attorney, which has only made people more curious about how long the divorce was in the works.

In this article, we'll take a look at how Tori Spelling's children were affected by her intense marriage to Dean McDermott. We'll also look at what we know about Spelling visiting an attorney and whether, at the time of this writing, her divorce is final.

Tori Spelling's children were affected by her difficult relationship with Dean McDermott because Spelling and McDermottexperienced financial problems. The couple's relationship took many twists and turns, which must have been tough for Spelling's children to deal with. In 2018, Life & Style Magazine reported that worry about money made Spelling and McDermott's family life tough.

Inside Tori Spelling's Attorney Visits

For several years, there were rumors about Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott getting divorced, and there were often news stories about Spelling at her attorney's office. Is Tori Spelling Still Married?

Since Tori Spelling filed for divorce in March 2024, according to Today.com, Spelling and Dean McDermott are still married at the time of this writing.

It's unclear how long the divorce proceedings will take, and since they will be working out custody of their kids, that might take a while. According to People, Spelling wants joint legacy and sole physical custody of their five children. Depending on the agreement, their divorce will definitely affect their five kids since they might see Spelling more often than McDermott.
