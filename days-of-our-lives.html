Shawn Christian

Shawn Christian attended Rogers High School in Wyoming, Michigan and from there he went on to Ferris St. University in Big Rapids, Michigan. He moved to Chicago to get into acting and starred in various stage productions. He quickly landed roles on television and made his debut in daytime on "As The World Turns" as sexy Mike Kasnoff until 1997 until he moved to Los Angeles and began acting in prime time series such as "Charmed" and "Summerland". Christian is also a film actor, having acting in several flicks such as "Beautiful" and "Meet Dave".

Shawn is married to Deborah, with whom he met auditioning for a print ad commercial in Chicago 1991. They married May 18, 1996. Shawn enjoys water sports and resides in Los Angeles with his wife Deborah and son Kameron who was born July 2000. He also has a step daughter, Taylor Quinn Cole, model and actress.
