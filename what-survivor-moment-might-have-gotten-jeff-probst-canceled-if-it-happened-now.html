What Survivor moment might have gotten Jeff Probst canceled if it happened now?

During an episode of Survivor: The Amazon, there was an endurance challenge that saw the players competing to see who would stand on top of a pole the longest. At various points in the challenge, Jeff Probst offered the players bribes to come down and lose. At one point in the challenge, Jenna Morasca and Heidi Strobel offered to lose the challenge and strip off their clothes in return for peanut butter and chocolate. A seemingly excited Probst took them up on this offer and the two young ladies followed through on their part of the deal. If Probst pretty much bribed two young women to take off their clothes on camera today, that very likely would get him canceled.
