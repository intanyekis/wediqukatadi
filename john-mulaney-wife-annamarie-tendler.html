John Mulaney wife, Annamarie Tendler Bio, Wedding, Married Life, Net Worth.

Annamarie Tendler is the ex-wife of John Mulaney but to introduce her with her ex-husband name will not justify her popularity. She is an American makeup artist, writer, and founder of the Daily Face featuring on her Tumbler blog (Annamarie.tumblr.com). She has worked as a makeup artist and hairstylist for over a decade before giving full time on Victorian lampshades. She has published two books: The Daily Face: 25 Makeup Looks for Day, Night, and Everything In Between! And Pin It!: 20 Fabulous Bobby Pin Hairstyles. Her current age is 38.

Early life and Education

Annamarie Tendler was born on June 9, 1985, in Connecticut, U.S.. She spent her childhood as a free-spirited girl enjoying every moment without the burden of the world. As she grew up her responsibility level also increased which made her more serious than before.

She attended high school in her hometown and graduated in 2003. After that, she moved to New York City to pursue her passion for hairstyling and makeup. She enrolled in the Vidal Sassoon and the Make-up Designory, where she learned the skills and techniques of professional hair and makeup artists. She also studied photography at the Parsons School of Design, but dropped out after a year. She later resumed her education at The New School, where she earned her bachelor’s degree in writing and psychology. In 2021, she completed her master’s degree in costume studies from NYU Steinhardt, with a thesis on the lip filler phenomenon and postfeminist female sexuality. Tendler’s education reflects her diverse and creative interests, as well as her academic achievements.

Career

She started working from her early 20s as a hairstylist and makeup artist in New York. She has a long list of television series and comedy specials on which she has worked including The Old Man and the Seymour, CollegeHumor Originals, John Mulaney: New in Town, Aziz Ansari: Dangerously Delicious and John Mulaney: The Comeback Kid. Till now she has worked in films, fashion, and television.

She uses her unique style and coolest ideas of the makeover that can turn any fiction scene no less than a reality. She has also partnered with MTV style to share ways of makeup process and tips to a global audience. Being an expert in her field, many magazines including Glamour, DailyCandy and HelloGiggles has published her tutorials. She also featured as host in the show The Other side.

She started blogging from December of 2007. Her blog “The Daily Face” on Tumblr is hugely popular and has inspired hundreds of thousands of followers. She mainly helps to achieve the looks that women are dying to have one with simple daily makeup routine.

Annamarie Tendler has written two books on makeup and hairstyle. The Daily Face: 25 Makeup Looks for Day, Night, and Everything In Between was published in 2014. Her second book, Pin It! 20 Fabulous Bobby Pin Hairstyles, was published in 2017.

But now seems that she has changed the subject of her decoration from enlightening the face to decorating the lamp. She makes each lampshade entirely by hand using both new and vintage fabrics, trims, and fringe.

Why she changed her career path?

She was in the process of decorating her house and searching for a Victorian lampshade. But unfortunately, she did not find any perfect lampshade that would fit in her house. After intensive searching, she decided to shift her efforts towards creating. She then ordered the DVD after the suggestion of her husband to teach herself how to make lampshades by hand. She accepted that the first one she made was not good and threw it in the dustbin but after some practice, she was happy with her work on the new lamp. She now owns Silk Parlor that sells handmade Victorian lampshades which are mostly one of a kind and vintage for upwards of $1,000. Customers can order their own unique lampshades.

Mulaney often joked about his relationship with Tendler in his stand-up specials, praising her honesty, confidence, and creativity.
