Who is Lil Scrappy?

Lil Scrappy is an American rapper. He is best known for the singles “Head Bussa” and “No Problems.”

Since 2012, he appears in the second installment of the Love & Hip Hop reality television franchise Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Lil Scrappy: Professional Life and Career

Lil Scrappy has been an active member of the industry since 2002. His 2004 collaborative album with Trillville went gold and he became famous in media. He released his debut album Bred 2 Die Born 2 Live on May 5, 2006.

The album features appearances by Yung Joc, Three 6 Mafia, Lil Jon, Young Buck, 50 Cent, Olivia, Young Dro, and Bohanon. His single Money in the Bank became his biggest solo hit and most commercially successful single in the US.

His second album, Prince of the South was released on May 13, 2008, through Real Talk Entertainment. Scrappy’s second independent album, Prince of the South 2 was released on October 19, 2010.

The second single from the album entitled “No Love” gained over 100,000 views on YouTube in its first week.
