A Peek Inside Kawhi Leonard's $17 Million LA Mansion

Quick Links

  • An Estimated Price Tag Of $25 Million 
  • The Immaculate Beach-Like Interiors
  • A Subterranean Party Room With Private Theatre And Bar

The Los Angeles Clippers superstar is known for frequently trading in real estate; this time, he bought himself 11 815 square feet of paradise in the Pacific Palisades neighborhood of LA. He owns a $7 million penthouse next to the Staples Center and a $13 million mansion in San Diego. With the current luxurious purchase, his total real estate dealings are worth $37 million. Nestled between hills, the gorgeous white and gray structure merges into the scenic landscape. It has all the modern amenities for family living and entertaining.

A $103 million contract with the Los Angeles Clippers allows Kawhi to easily invest in mega-million real estate properties.

An Estimated Price Tag Of $25 Million 

According to Dirt, the seven ensuite bedrooms and twelve bathrooms luxury abode was built for Hollywood elite producer Ryan Kavanaugh. He custom-built the mansion according to Hamptons-style affluent decor. The construction was completed in 2018, and by February 2019, it was up on the real estate market for an asking price of $25 million. It stayed on the market for two years and was taken off. Later, once back up with an asking price of $17.5 million, it was immediately purchased by Leonard for $17 million. The property boasts 11,815 square feet of primary residence on 82 acres with panoramic ocean views. It comes with manicured gardens, a dramatic driveway, and a gray-tone exterior complementing the surroundings.

The wow factor of the multi-million dollar mega mansion is that it sits on top of a peninsula offering sweeping views of the Pacific ocean. The Hamptons style cannot be missed in the interiors, and the whitewashed walls and sophisticated decor are reminiscent of a classic beach vibe with unhindered views of the majestic blue ocean and the Santa Monica Conservancy from every room. A whimsical white picket fence greets guests with delicate rose bushes at the entrance. Inspired by grand vacation homes, the interiors are bright and airy with a coastal vibe. Large French-style windows let in ample natural light, and the simplicity of the decor mimics Rococo, Empire, and Baroque designs. The walled and gated Pacific Palisades community is an excellent private spot for the famed NBA star to relax with family and friends.

The Immaculate Beach-Like Interiors

A dual-sided colossal fireplace separates the living room from the entertainment area. Imposing glass doors give access to pocket rooms and powder rooms and lead directly to the stylish patio surrounding the main level of the mansion. According to Sacbee, the three-storied mega mansion comes with a subterranean level entertaining area built for fun and hosting many guests. Hardwood floors run throughout the home, with high ceilings decorated with ornate pendants and ceiling lighting. The palatial primary suite comes with a private balcony that opens up to striking ocean views and runs along the entire width of the main bathroom. An elegant fireplace adorns the center of the room, and soft furnishings in neutral tones and white walls complement the classic decor. The double-sided main bath is connected with a romantic glass-walled shower at the center. Custom built-in shelving units and his/her massive walk-in closets feature inside the primary suite.

According to Architectural Digest, the gigantic living space imbibes outdoor/indoor living with folding glass doors. Large contemporary paintings adorn the white walls, almost like an art gallery display. A large tufted ottoman anchors the earthy-toned sofas in the secondary living room. The mansion is equipped with a mirror-walled gym for fitness enthusiasts, and six family bedrooms are located on the top level, with the butler’s pantry and the maid rooms at the ground level.

A Subterranean Party Room With Private Theatre And Bar

The fully automated mansion has an insane entertainment space below the main living quarters. According to CA Sports, the grand area boasts a gray-toned 10-seater movie theater, a man cave with a billiards gaming room for leisure, and a massive 960-bottle wine cellar with a tasting bar. Guests can enjoy the fresh air and stunning views of the rambling hills while outdoor dining is on the upper level. A gourmet-style white kitchen with modern appliances makes for an ideal chef’s kitchen. Marble countertops and white cabinetry are the main features of the spacious cooking space.

Large wooden decks on neatly cut lawns offer a unique entertainment experience for lazy summer evenings. The decks are adorned with modern wicker furniture and comfortable sofas. The main outdoor selling point is the large infinity pool right down a slope. It offers an adjoining enclosed cabana for relaxation with spectacular views. Nestled amid tall trees and manicured green grass, the swimming pool is a paradise spot with ample privacy.

Leonards's third property is breathtaking and offers exclusivity since it is situated on the remote corner of Westside. A colossal investment by the rising All-Star NBA player, this property is fit for royalty.
