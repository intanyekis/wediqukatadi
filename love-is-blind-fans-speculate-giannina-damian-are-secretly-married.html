Fans Speculate Giannina &amp; Damian Are Secretly Married

Love may be blind, but it also keeps us guessing. At least that’s true for season 1 couple Giannina and Damian, who got engaged without ever laying eyes on each other. Since their  initial engagement, the pair has traveled to Mexico, moved in and even broken up at their own wedding. Yes, we are talking about that time that Damian dumped Giannina at the altar in front of all their friends and family.

Somehow these two love birds came back from that blow up, and they actually got back together just hours after their relatives scurried out of the wedding. But in recent months, G and D have disappeared from each other’s social media accounts, fueling rumors of a breakup.

Some fans, however, have another theory about our favorite Love Is Blind couple.

Giannina And Damian’s Big Secret

Over the course of summer 2020, Giannina and Damian were busy posting photos of each other on their respective Instagram accounts. However, those cute couples posts stopped abruptly in the fall.

Some fans speculated that the pair had parted ways, after Damian was caught on camera walking arm-in-arm with a scantily-clad Francesca Farago (Yes, she is the girl who couldn’t go a day without kissing her beau on Too Hot To Handle).

At the time, Giannina denied that she and Damian were headed for a breakup. Nonetheless, in recent months, speculation about a possible separation has increased, as neither G nor D posted a V-Day pic.

Now, however, there is a new rumor going around: Giannina and Damian are secretly married.

In her post recent post, G shares some videos of a recent manicure. Naturally, these images did display her hands. And low and behold: Giannina’s ring finger is not bare! So, what does this mean?

One fan expressed her shock and confusion at Giannina’s choice of jewelry. “You have a wedding ring set on,” the Love Is Blind viewer pointed out, “I’m so confused on her and Damian.”

In Giannina’s previous photo, a fan identified our girl’s bling as, “the engagement ring (Damian) gave her,” and insinuated that they couldn’t be broken up if G is still wearing it.

Still, many fans are asking: “Did the two break up?”

We will have to wait for them to make a statement to know for sure.
