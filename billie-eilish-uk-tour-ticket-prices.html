Ticket Prices For Billie Eilish UK Tour Elicits Anger From Fans

The global pandemic has paused and canceled many major events, inclusive of big concerts that fans were greatly looking forward to.  One of the canceled events was the 2019/2020's Where Do We Go? tour that Billie Eilish was set to embark on. She was delighted to take to social media to announce that things are opening up, and she's going to be hitting the road again for a UK tour. Billie Eilish was delighted to announce that her  Happier Than Ever tour is getting the green light, and she proceeded in releasing additional details to her massive fan following. Capital FM reports that unfortunately, the excitement quickly shifted to anger when fans realized that the ticket prices were astronomically priced.

Fans of all different types of music genres are beyond excited to see the return of live concerts come to their local area once again. The world is slowly starting to reopen and as part of the pandemic recovery plan, there are a slew of concerts that have begun to resume their planning stages, giving fans renewed hope. The future is looking brighter with each concert announcement, and the big one that was just announced was none other than the UK leg of Billie Eilish's tour.

Eilish has been tantalizing fans with the release of her Happier Than Ever album, and they're ready to see the talented young artist showcase her skills on a live stage.

Millions of people across the globe are itching to get out and re-join the major concert scene and this seemed like a fabulous concert to attend, until the cost of the tickets was revealed.

The UK publication, Express relayed the anger expressed by furious fans when Eilish revealed the actual sticker price for attendance at this big event. "Prices for the Manchester events begin at £46.30 and go all the way up to £100. Meanwhile, her Birmingham tickets kick-off at £39 and go up to £90." For those looking to make a quick calculation, that's nearly $150 USD for very generic seats to this concert.

Frustration is building as fans try their best to come out of the pandemic with hopeful enthusiasm, only to realize that their wallets are going to take a huge hit if they want to attend this particular concert.

The demand for ticket remains, and Eilish has certainly racked up some serious hardware since her last concert, presumably adding to the list of reasons as to why the cost per ticket has been driven up so greatly.

There has been no response from Eilish's team about the anger and frustration that fans are feeling over the inflated concert costs.
