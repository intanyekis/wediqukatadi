Tatyana Childress, Who Was Missing On Sunday, Was Found Dead

Tatyana Childress is a 21-year-old who was reported to be missing. However, she was dead when the authorities discovered her. 

It was reported by Guilford County Sheriff’s Office that the woman who was missing was discovered dead on Huffine Mill Road in McLeansville at 1 PM.

Tatyana’s mother said her daughter went missing on Sunday. 

During the investigation, the sheriff’s police said there was no foul play detected.

The first person to discover Tatyana’s dead body was a homeowner in McLeansville. The homeowner also said her car was detected. 

The homeowner added that when he came back to his home from a weekend away, he discovered tire marks on his yard and suspected that someone might’ve broken into his house.

He said he didn’t find anything in his house which could create the possibility of a break-in, but when he looked at his backyard, it looked as if someone had rammed into it. 

He also added that he saw a car in his backyard, and when he went closer to it, he saw that the car was a black Honda Accord.

Childress was in her car, and the car crashed into his pond. The homeowner immediately called the 911 operator and informed them about the incident. 

According to sources, Tatyana was last sighted on Burlington Road in Greensboro. Her location was found to be at a Sheetz gas station.

This place was a mile away from where her dead body was discovered.  

Childress’ mother said she is still waiting for the details of the cause of her daughter’s death. She was asked to tell if she knew why her daughter was in that area, but she replied she didn’t know.
