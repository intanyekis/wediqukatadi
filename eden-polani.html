Eden Polani: Leonardo DiCaprios Alleged 19-Year-Old Girlfriend

  • She was seen hanging out with superstar Leonardo DiCaprio at a music release party.
  • Neither DiCaprio nor Polani has passed any comments regarding the dating rumors. 

Eden Polani’s relationship status has become the talk of the town recently. She was seen hanging out with superstar Leonardo DiCaprio at a music release party, which put people under the impression that they were dating. 

When it comes to the relationship status of celebrities, there is often much more to the equation than meets the eye. 

Such was the case when Leonardo DiCaprio, 48, was photographed at Ebony Riley’s new music release party in Los Angeles on January 31st, 2023, with 19-year-old model Eden Polani. 

Although rumors of a relationship erupted immediately, a reliable source close to DiCaprio has since told E! News that the two are not an item. 

The truth behind their relationship remains underlying speculation and open for theories and debate.

“Leo and Eden just so happen to have been seated next to each other at the party and were hanging in the same group,” the source said. “Just because Leo might be talking to or sitting with a girl doesn’t mean he’s dating her.”

Neither DiCaprio nor Polani has passed any comments regarding the dating rumors. Recently, Polani deleted all her posts off Instagram, which has left fans wondering about the status of her relationship with DiCaprio. 

The model is currently facing a lot of backlash for her rumored relationship with the Hollywood actor, who is more than twice her age.

Eden Polani is a Paris-born, 19-year-old model currently living in Los Angeles. She was born on March 21, 2003. 

She has over 220k followers on Instagram. Her following skyrocketed soon after she was photographed with DiCaprio. Polani often posts pictures of herself while traveling to posh countries like Switzerland. 

Eden Polani is originally from Israel and featured on multiple Israeli covers. Her Instagram bio proudly displays the Israeli flag.

The model does not have an official Twitter or TikTok account. Before her latest rumored affair, Polani was linked to 31-year-old Gil Ofer, the son of billionaire Idan Ofer. Their romance was swept under the carpet due to Polani’s age. 

Her family details have not been shared with the public. Polani has supposedly attended high school in her hometown, but the school’s name has yet to be discovered. 

She, however, did not attend college probably because she wanted to become a professional model. Eden Polani’s current estimated net worth is $600,000. 

She is a professional Israeli fashion and runway model, social media personality, and Instagram influencer.

Eden has been featured in high fashion magazines, photoshoots, and advertising. Next Management in The United Kingdom represents Polani, Why Not Model in Italy, Maddison in France, Kult Models in Germany, and Trend Models in Spain.
