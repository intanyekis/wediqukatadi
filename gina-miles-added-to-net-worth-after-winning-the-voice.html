How Gina Miles Added To Her Net Worth After Winning The Voice

Highlights

  • Winning The Voice Season 23 has significantly increased Gina Miles' net worth.
  • Since her victory, Miles has been actively pursuing a career in the entertainment industry.
  • Miles has gained a growing following on social media, with over 100,000 Instagram followers.

19-year-old Gina Miles won The Voice on May 23rd, 2023, bringing more fame and fortune to the young, talented performer. Miles was coached by music superstar, Niall Horan of One Direction fame, who has focused on a solo career since the group's breakup.

Since Miles won The Voice, she has remained musically active and is pursing a career in the entertainment industry. She is also taking opportunities to increase her net worth.

How Has Gina Miles' Net Worth Increased Since Winning The Voice Season 23?

According to Billboard, Gina Miles won $100,000 and a recording contract with Universal Music Group upon winning Season 23 of The Voice. Miles' Voice winnings certainly enhanced the singer's financial well-being. Furthermore, her recording contract deal is just the start of what could be a long and lucrative career for Miles.

Following her The Voice victory, Miles started to make public appearances. In June 2023, Miles performed her rendition of the United States's national anthem, "The Star-Spangled Banner", for a group of graduating firefighters in her home city of Sacramento, California, according to NBC News.

Miles spoke about the experience in an interview:

"I'm really happy to be home and see everyone and to do things like this with our community. It's important and exciting."

While Miles probably did not get paid for her performance, she received more recognition in her community as a rising music star. Besides her performance in Sacramento, she has begun to start touring as a music act. In September 2023, Miles and Edwin McCain opened for Jewel at her The Venue at Thunder Valley Casino and Resort concert. While Miles's salary from the performance is not known, she most likely earned income from her appearance.

As Miles records more music, it can be assumed that she will tour more. She may remain an opening act as her career blossoms. Fans have wondered if Miles may ever join former coach, Niall Horan, as an opening act. According to E!. Horan is on board.

"If we can get her on some shows, things will have to happen in the background, but absolutely yeah....This girl is like, you know talent when you...hear it. And this girl's got it, so yeah, there's no reason why she wouldn't open up for me at some point."

Miles has spoken highly about working with Horan and shared publicly how great of a mentor he was to her. A potential collaboration or touring together could very well happen.

While Miles has not released original music since her victory as of November 2023, she did give fans a preview of an original composition entitled "Someone New" in July 2023 on Instagram, according to NBC News. To introduce the song, Miles wrote: "Wanted to share a little original song i've been working on, this one's Something New." Miles has released music in the past, particularly before she appeared and won The Voice Season 23.

Miles has spoken about her vision for future music in an NBC interview after her The Voice victory. She stated that:

"I feel like I want to experiment with sounds and see what we like. And I feel like I've definitely found my lane through the show of those more darker, moodier songs. Also pop songs. I really do enjoy a pop moment, and I like performing and having fun just as much as I like to be emotional and have those deeper songs, too."

Since her victory on The Voice, Miles has gained an impressive number of social media followers. As of November 2023, she has more than one hundred thousand Instagram followers on her official account. She is gaining a following, and this should only continue as she releases new music and tours.

While Miles's net worth is not publicly known, she is most likely already a millionaire, according to the Wealthy Spy. This cannot be confirmed, however, and is only an estimate. Based on interviews given after her The Voice victory, Miles is optimistic and excited about the career that lies in front of her. She has the full support of her family, and is living out her dreams.

From a high school choir singer to winning The Voice Season 23, Gina Miles has accomplished a lot for her young age. Through The Voice, not only has she earned fans, but has built valuable celebrity connections, including her coach, Niall Horan. She is signed with Republic Records of the Universal Music Group, which is an excellent start for the aspiring star.

How Did Gina Miles Get Into The Music Industry?

Gina Miles was born in November 2003 in Paxton, Illinois. According to Hollywood Life, she grew up with a single father, who happened to be a part-time disc jockey. Growing up surrounded by music, Miles wanted to learn more about the industry and assist her father with his DJing. After joining the high school choir at the Paxton-Buckley-Loda High School, Miles recognized that she had a gift for singing and wanted to pursue a singing career further.

Knowing that California is a hub for entertainment, Miles, with her father's approval, decided to move to Sacramento after her junior year of high school to pursue a music career. She moved in with an aunt, according to her official The Voice biography. Her first EP, "Who Are You", was released in January 2022 independently, consisting of five songs:

  • "I Can Feel It"
  • "Fever"
  • "Incredibly Impatient"
  • "Cherry Pie"
  • "End With You"

In 2023, Miles decided to take her greatest step into the industry by auditioning for The Voice. Fortunately, Miles's talent shinned, and she was able to prove herself as a professional performer. With the guidance of Horan, Miles grew, evolved, and became more confident as she wowed her audiences with every performance. Miles's decision to audition paid off spectacularly and launched her career.
