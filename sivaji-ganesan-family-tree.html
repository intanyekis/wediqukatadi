Sivaji Ganesan Wiki and Bio

Sivaji Ganesan Wiki and Bio: - Ganesan was born in Villupuram, India, on October 1, 1928, as the fourth son of Chinnaiya Manrayar and Rajamani Ammal. Ganesan began his career under the stage name V. C. Ganesan. According to media reports, the initial 'V' refers to Villupuram, however, one of Ganesan's sons claims it stands for Vettaithidal, the hamlet from whence their family comes. Ganesan chose to join a touring stage acting company at the age of seven, against his father's wishes. He came to Tiruchirappalli at the age of ten and joined a theatrical group in Sangiliyandapuram, where he began to appear in stage plays.
