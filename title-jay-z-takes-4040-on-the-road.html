Jay Z Takes 40/40 on the Road

Jay-Z is set to open a new 40/40 club in Atlantic City.

According to Rodney Jerkins, “Atlantic City hasn’t been hot since Mike Tyson vs. [Michael] Spinks in 1986.”

Jay’s about to change all of that.

After jets and limos picked up his guests, Jigga opened the 40/40 club in style. With party goers like Diddy, Destiny’s Child and Barry Bonds, Jay attracted plenty of celebrities to the shindig. It looked like a Hollywood night.

So, why Atlantic City?

“A.C. was just the next step,”Jay said before adding, “New York and New Jersey are neighbors, and there’s just a whole development going on out here.”

So, how do the mayor and governor feel about this? “I spoke with the mayor and governor and everybody was just so happy to have me here,” he added.

Jay hopes to heighten the popularity of Atlantic City, and bring in many tourists.

“It means that Atlantic City is not just a gambling town; it’s a place where you can come club and shop,”Jigga said.

Michelle Williams, a guest at the event understands what Jay is talking about when speaking of Atlantic City.

“I heard somebody say Atlantic City is officially sexy, and it is. When you walk in the 40/40, it’s just a sexy vibe. The ambience is great, the food is good. Everybody feels comfortable, and it’s good to finally have that in Atlantic City.”

Rodney Jerkins, also a guest, was impressed by this and hopes for a future business relationship with Jay-Z.

“We haven’t had a red carpet like this in over 20 years. I want to talk to Jay about doing a hotel together. This man’s got a vision. He understands ultra-sexy. He understands hip-hop. He understands what’s now and what’s tomorrow. When you know all those things, you can’t lose.”

Others also see the vision Jay has. Serena Williams mentioned that she was “inspired” by Jay.

She says, “He’s an entrepreneur. He started from so from very little and has so much. I’m inspired by him because I’m on that same road. I started from very little, and I’m aspiring to be like him.”

So, what else is in store for Jay? Some rumors are floating around of a marriage or a child with Beyonce, but none of that is confirmed.

Plans for another 40/40 club have been confirmed. “It’s not set in stone, but it’s pretty much 80 percent done,”Jay said of a possible Los Angeles location.

Jigga is tight lipped about other plans for the future.

“Y’all know every time I come out, it’s going to be something special. It’s going to be a moment. Everybody is going to have a good time,” he said.
