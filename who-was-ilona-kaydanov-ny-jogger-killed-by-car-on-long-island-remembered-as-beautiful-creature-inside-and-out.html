Who was Ilona Kaydanov? NY jogger killed by car on Long Island remembered as 'beautiful creature ins

LONG ISLAND, NEW YORK: A 22-year-old jogger died after being struck by a car on a Long Island road on Thursday morning. While Ilona Kaydanov was killed her two younger brothers survived with injuries.

Kaydanov, a gifted dancer and graduate of Stony Brook University, and her siblings were struck by a 2022 Chrysler Voyager around 8:20 in the morning while running on the northbound shoulder of Harned Road in Commack, according to Suffolk County police, as reported by New York Post.

Where was Ilona Kaydanov declared dead?

Kaydanov, a resident of Commack, was taken to hospital in critical condition and declared dead at South Shore University Hospital.

Dennis, 20, and Joseph Kaydanov, 13, her younger brothers, were transported with non-life-threatening wounds to Good Samaritan University Hospital.
