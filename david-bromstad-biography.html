Who is David Bromstad?

David Bromstad is a famous American interior designer and television personality.

He became popular after winning the debut season of HGTV’s Design Star in 2006.

David is best known as the host of his own television show, Color Splash(which has crossed over 100 episodes), My Lottery Dream Home.
