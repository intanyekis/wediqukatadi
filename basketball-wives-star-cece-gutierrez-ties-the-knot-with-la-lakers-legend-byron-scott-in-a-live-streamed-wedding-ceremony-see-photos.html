Basketball Wives star, CeCe Gutierrez ties the knot with LA Lakers legend Byron Scott in a live-s


The union of Basketball Wives’ star, CeCe Gutierrez, and former Los Angeles Lakers guard, Byron Scott, has been sealed in matrimony.

Celebrating their love, the 47-year-old reality star and the 59-year-old former NBA player and coach exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony over the weekend. The wedding was live-streamed to enable their friends and family to participate from their homes due to the restrictions imposed by the Coronavirus pandemic.

For her special day, CeCe looked stunning in a mermaid-style white gown designed by Netta BenShabu, while her husband, Scott, appeared dapper in a black suit with a matching bowtie. Both the bride and groom adhered to safety measures by wearing masks during the ceremony.

Gutierrez and Scott had to postpone their original wedding date due to the COVID-19 pandemic. However, the couple decided not to delay their union any further.
