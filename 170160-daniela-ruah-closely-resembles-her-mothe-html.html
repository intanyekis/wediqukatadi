Daniela Ruah Closely Resembles Her Mother in Recent Instagram Post

Actress Daniela Ruah and her otologist mother share a striking resemblance and fans can not stop pointing it out. It seems good looks are genetic in the Ruah family.

The Portuguese-American actress, Daniela Ruah is best known for her role on "NCIS: LA." She plays Special Agent Kensi Blye.

However, she is making headlines for a different reason recently. One of her Instagram posts has caught the attention of fans everywhere.

Daniela Ruah attends a photocall for the TV series 'NCIS: Los Angeles' during the 52nd Monte Carlo TV Festival | Getty Images

The star took to Instagram to wish her mother Happy Birthday. She posted a picture of the two of them smiling happily at the camera.

Fans were blown away by how closely the mother-daughter duo resembled each other. From their brilliant pearly white smiles to their dark locks and face shape, the pair look nearly identical.

Ruah herself acknowledged and poked fun at their similar looks in her caption. In her message to her mother she wrote:

"Happy Birthday to this ray of light and my younger sister! Love you, mom!"

A fan comment on Daniela Ruah's post | Instagram: @danielaruah

Fans agreed with her completely and many pointed out that they could be sisters. Many also wished her mom a Happy Birthday.

Instagram comment left by a fan on Daniela Ruah's post to her mother | Instagram: @danielaruah

The stunning picture garnered over forty-five thousand likes and multiple comments agreeing or echoing each other. It's not the first time fans have been given a glimpse of her mother. In 2012, her mother joined her on an episode of NCIS.

The Ruah looks and genes have proved consistently strong. Earlier this year, Daniela and her daughters stunned fans with how similar they looked as well. When she shared pics of the girls, many fans pointed out that they definitely inherited her stunning smile.
