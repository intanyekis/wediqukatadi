Tyler Christopher Career: Movies and TV shows

In 1993, Tyler auditioned for the role of Stone Cates for the soap opera General Hospital. He signed a three-year deal, and he played the role of Nikolas Cassadine for the serial on April 1996.

On March 2011, Soap Opera Digest reported on their social media accounts that Tyler was released from the serial, due to his casting on ABC Family’s The Lying Game, produced by former General Hospital head writer Charles Pratt, Jr.

On March 2013, Tyler returned to General Hospital, while continuing to appear on The Lying Game. He had signed a deal to appear on a regular basis on General Hospital on June 2013. In 2016 September, Soap Opera Digest reported that he would not be reprising his portrayal of Nikolas.

On August 2017, Tyler joined the cast of Days of Our Lives. On December 29, 2017, he made his first appearance as Stefan DiMera.
