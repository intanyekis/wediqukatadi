Joe Manganiello Kids: Insights Into His Personal Life

Were Joe Manganiello Kids a factor in the end of his marriage with Sofia Vergara? Joe Manganiello and Sofía Vergara’s seven-year marriage recently ended, partly because they were unable to have children together. 

A source revealed that Vergara had desired to have a child with Manganiello, but they ultimately agreed that their age made it impractical.

The insider claimed that they had been trying to conceive a baby since their wedding in 2015. 

However, Vergara was hesitant to use in vitro fertilization (IVF) after her terrible experience with her ex-fiancé Nick Loeb.

Sofia Vergara had undergone IVF treatments with Loeb in 2013, which had resulted in two frozen embryos. 

After their break up, Loeb fought to keep them; however, the court eventually ruled in Vergara’s favor.

In 2021, the court granted Vergara’s request for a Permanent Injunction. It restricted Loeb from creating a child without explicit written permission from Vergara.

The source emphasized that Vergara took a lot out of the IVF process. She didn’t want Loeb to raise children that were biologically half hers.

Given her unpleasant experience with IVF, Vergara refused to use the same method as Manganiello. However, the couple had been trying to conceive naturally without success.

Announcing the divorce

The couple had been growing apart for a while before their divorce. 

An insider disclosed that they had been “living separate lives” for quite some time. They were both focused on their careers and loved ones.

According to the insider, Vergara had been giving her all to her new beauty brand, TOTY, and was very involved and excited about it. 

She hosted an event in Los Angeles at the end of June to celebrate the launch. 

However, Manganiello was not present. Vergara had a framed photo of them kissing in her home, indicating that she still had feelings for him.

Vergara and Manganiello announced their divorce on July 17, 2021. In a joint statement, they politely asked for respect of their privacy as they navigate this new phase of their lives.

Despite the news of their separation, it was not a total surprise that the two were having issues. A source close to the couple disclosed that they had been taking some distance from each other to contemplate their future.

The news of their divorce shocked fans who saw them as the ultimate power couple. They were known for showing affection towards one another publicly.

Joe Manganiello kids: The significance of children

Children are usually considered a significant aspect of a couple’s life, especially in long-term relationships. However, for various reasons, some couples are unable to have them, leading to discord in the relationship.

Fertility issues often place a significant burden on marriages. According to the American Society for Reproductive Medicine, about ten percent of women in the US struggle to conceive.

Final Thoughts

Joe Manganiello and Sofía Vergara’s divorce has caused a lot of reactions from fans and the public.

While the couple offered no particular motive for their separation, their inability to have children was clearly a significant factor.
