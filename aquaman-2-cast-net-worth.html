While Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom may not have breached the surface with box office dominance like its predecessor, one aspect remains captivating: the financial depths reached by its cast.

Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom trawled a global gross of $118.4 million despite mixed reviews, and still generated a tidal wave of paychecks for its stars from its production budget of $215 million:

Jason Momoa, wielding the trident as Aquaman, stands as the film's highest earner, after netting $15 million for the sequel – which is double what he earned for the first film.

This princely sum adds to his Momoa's net worth (estimated at $50 million), buoyed by brand endorsements and businesses.

Nicole Kidman, who portrayed Atlanna – Arthur and Orm's mother, and the former queen of Atlantis – is the second highest-paid member of the Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom cast, with reported earnings of $5 million.

From seasoned pros like Dolph Lundgren to rising stars like Yahya Abdul-Mateen II, the Aquaman 2 cast's diverse net worth paints a fascinating financial portrait.

9 Amber Heard

Amber Heard's Net Worth (Estimated): $500,000

Reprising her role as the fiery Mera in Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom, Amber Heard navigated complex underwater battles and, unfortunately, real-life legal battles, contributing to her current estimated net worth of $500,000.

A Note About Amber Heard's Net Worth: Most references to Amber Heard's net worth are derived from a single, widely-repeated source.

Nevertheless, Heard's reported pay from myriad projects points to a higher figure, and the impact of her high-profile legal battle on her finances is not expressly clear.

While a far cry from some of her cast mates, Heard boasts a diverse career spanning over 20 film and television credits, from her breakout role in All The Boys Love Mandy Lane to action flicks like Drive Angry And Never Back Down.

Heard reportedly earned $200,000 per episode to star in the nine-episode series The Stand, for a total payday of $1.8 million.

However, Heard's paycheck from the first Aquaman netted her $1 million, with the sequel adding another $2 million to her coffers.

Beyond acting, Heard has partnered with brands like L'Oréal, in a deal worth over $1.625 million for a two-year contract. However, recent legal entanglements have undoubtedly impacted Heard's financial landscape.

According to statements in Depp v. Heard lawsuits, Amber Heard earned $10 million before taxes from all sources (salaries, endorsements) from 2013 to 2019. Her peak earning year was said to be 2019, at around $3 million.

After a highly publicized defamation case filed by Johnny Depp, initially appeared to be on the hook for millions in damages, adding financial tension to an already complex career path; in response, Amber Heard's net worth was widely reported in the "negative millions."

In June 2022, The Hollywood Reporter indicated a jury ordered Heard to pay Depp $15 million in damages, but the two parties eventually settled for $1 million.

The settlement was reluctant on Heard's part, and in a lengthy statement, she said in part:

"It’s important for me to say that I never chose this. I defended my truth and in doing so my life as I knew it was destroyed. The vilification I have faced on social media is an amplified version of the ways in which women are re-victimized when they come forward. Now I finally have an opportunity to emancipate myself from something I attempted to leave over six years ago and on terms I can agree to. I have made no admission, this is not an act of concession. There are no restrictions or gags with respect to my voice moving forward."

8 Yahya Abdul-Mateen II

Yahya Abdul-Mateen II's Net Worth (Estimated): $2.5 Million

Emerging from the depths of Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom as the menacing Black Manta, Yahya Abdul-Mateen II continues to make waves in Hollywood.

Abdul Mateen II's net worth was most recently estimated at $2.5 million in 2021, based on various roles in television and film. As such, Mateen II's net worth has likely risen considerably, in part due to his appearance in big projects like Aquaman 2.

Though his most recognized role may be alongside Jason Momoa, Abdul-Mateen II boasts a diverse filmography spanning over 15 credits.

His big break came through the Netflix musical drama The Get Down. Since then, he's graced the screen in blockbuster hits like Baywatch, The Greatest Showman, The Matrix Resurrections, and Damon Lindelof's Watchmen, which earned him an Emmy.

The superhero realm, however, proved a lucrative one. Abdul-Mateen II's second outing as Black Manta in Aquaman And The Lost Kingdomnetted him $750,000.

While business ventures and endorsements may not yet appreciably line his pockets, Abdul-Mateen II's trajectory is on a steady ascent.

With his charm, charisma, and undeniable acting chops, we can expect to see this talented actor surface in even bigger roles and further increase his financial depths in the years to come.

7 Pilou Asbæk

Pilou Asbæk's Net Worth (Estimated): $4 Million

From Danish television's political shark tank in Borgen to the iron-fisted reign of Euron Greyjoy in Game Of Thrones, Pilou Asbæk has carved a path of charismatic villains and captivating heroes.

In Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom, he portrays Kordax, adding another layer to his multi-faceted career. Over 50 credits later, with blockbusters like Ben-Hur, The Great Wall, and Ghost In The Shell under his belt, Asbæk has built a lucrative empire of cinematic magic.

Pilou Asbæk's net worth is a bit trickier to estimate in dollars (because he's Danish), but estimates range from an implausibly low $1 million to a more reasonable $5 million, and is most frequently pegged at around $4 million.

6 Temuera Morrison

Temuera Morrison's Net Worth (Estimated): $5 Million

In Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom, veteran actor Temuera Morrison reprises his role as Tom Curry, Arthur Curry's father. Morrison hails from New Zealand, and was reportedly paid $200,000 for the role.

As of January 2022, Temuera Morrison's net worth was estimated at around $5 million, which speaks volumes about a career spanning film, television, and even music.

As with Pilou Asbæk, determining Morrison's net worth is complicated by differing currencies and region-specific factors, as well as by subsequent work on projects like Aquaman 2.

Morrison is widely recognized for his roles in the Star Wars franchise, notably as Jango Fett. He has accumulated more than 70 acting credits throughout his illustrious career.

His multifaceted contributions extend beyond acting, including directing the 2016 short film The Lost Pearl and serving as an associate producer on the 2013 documentary miniseries The Life And Times Of Temuera Morrison.

Morrison's legacy extends far beyond Hollywood glitz. A revered figure in New Zealand cinema, he is a recipient of the prestigious "Order Of Merit."

He has also ventured into directing and music, adding further texture to his artistic tapestries.

5 Randall Park

Randall Park's Net Worth (Estimated): $5 Million

Randall Park, whose wit has charmed audiences from sitcoms to Marvel movies, navigates the depths of Atlantis as Dr. Stephen Shin in Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom.

Koimoi claimed that Randall Park earned $300,000 for his return as Dr. Stephen Shin in Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom, but we were unable to locate firm figures.

Park's comedic prowess is undeniable, showcased in his breakout role as Louis Huang in Fresh Off The Boat, and his co-written rom-com gem Always Be My Maybe.

He's no stranger to versatility, though, adeptly tackling dramatic roles like Agent Jimmy Woo in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Whether eliciting laughter or wielding intellectual prowess, Park proves a captivating force.

His return in Aquaman 2 promised an injection of humor and intelligence into the underwater saga, demonstrating his ability to thrive in any narrative terrain.

Randall Park's net worth has been estimated at $5 million, with some outlets placing it at $10 million.

4 Patrick Wilson

Patrick Wilson's Net Worth (Estimated): $7 Million

Patrick Wilson reprised his role as the formidable Orm in Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom.

The actor replaced Amber Heard as the second lead, and was paid a rumored $2 million for the role. His breakout role in the HBO miniseries Angels In America garnered critical acclaim and Golden Globe and Emmy nominations, setting the stage for a diverse filmography.

He has chilled audiences in the Insidious and Conjuring franchises, earning him a "King Of Scream" moniker. But his talents extend beyond terror, as evidenced by his roles in Oscar-nominated films like Little Children and Watchmen.

Patrick Wilson's net worth has long been estimated at around $7 million (since at least early 2022), but the sheer scope of his presence across the Conjuring franchise-slash-universe suggests that $7 million may be on the low side.

3 Dolph Lundgren

Dolph Lundgren's Net Worth (Estimated): $18 Million

Dolph Lundgren resurfaces in Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom, wielding the trident not as the imposing villain Drago, but as the cunning King Nereus, Mera's father.

According to Koimoi, the Swedish actor was paid $500,000 for reprising his role as underwater royalty. He landed his defining role in Rocky IV as the bone-crunching Ivan Drago, etching the infamous "I must break you" line into pop culture's memory.

Lundgren has steadily built a filmography of over 80 credits, from playing He-Man to battling Sylvester Stallone again in The Expendables franchise.

Beyond acting, he flexes his intellectual muscles as a chemical engineer with a master's degree and boasts a fourth-degree black belt in karate. These multifaceted skills keep him in demand, with recent ventures including directing and starring in his own films.

With fists of fury and a fortune to match, Dolph Lundgren's net worth has been estimated at $18 million by Celebrity Net Worth, and $18 million and $20 million by the site Equity Atlas.

Lundgren's net worth may be higher than those estimates, owing to the length of his career and extensive body of work.

2 Jason Momoa

Jason Momoa's Net Worth (Estimated): $40 Million

Aquaman isn't the only thing bringing in waves – Jason Momoa's net worth is currently estimated at around $40 million (up from $25 million not too long ago), proving he's built a whole ocean of success.

A Note About Jason Momoa's Net Worth: For most of 2023, Jason Momoa's net worth was reported at around $25 million, changing around the time Aquaman 2 was released.

That figure may have been calculated by adding Momoa's reported $15 million salary to his purported $25 million net worth; if so, that estimate does not account for myriad elements of an actor's net worth, like assets, management costs, investments, or diverse income streams.

From Baywatch lifeguard to Hollywood leading man, Momoa's journey started with modeling, before he landed his first acting role in Baywatch Hawaii.

That rugged charm soon caught bigger eyes, leading to his breakout as Ronon Dex in Stargate Atlantis and the unforgettable Khal Drogo in Game Of Thrones. But the real splash came when Momoa landed the coveted role of Aquaman in the DC Universe.

His fresh take on the role propelled the character to stardom, earning him $15 million for Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom alone, a major upgrade from the $7.5 million he was paid for the first film.

Momoa has tackled diverse roles in The Lego Movie 2, Netflix's action thriller Sweet Girl, and Denis Villeneuve's science fiction epic Dune. He even brings his muscles and action chops to Fast X.

Beyond the screen, Momoa makes waves for his own reasons. His passion for his Hawaiian heritage shines through in Mananalu Pure Water, an aluminum-canned water brand created to fight plastic pollution.

It's a reminder that this Aquaman cares just as much about the real world as he does about Atlantis.

1 Nicole Kidman

Nicole Kidman's Net Worth (Estimated): $250 Million

Nicole Kidman graced the screen as Atlanna, the former queen of Atlantis and mother to Arthur and Orm, in Aquaman And The Lost Kingdom.

Kidman's stellar performance earned her $5 million for the sequel, making her the second-highest-paid cast member after Jason Momoa.

The Academy Award winner is not only an accomplished actress but also a producer and philanthropist. Over the last several decades, she has consistently ranked among the highest-paid performers globally.

Notably, Kidman's earnings have soared, with reported figures such as the $10 million payday for Netflix's The Prom and a $1 million per-episode salary for HBO's The Undoing.

Her illustrious career includes blockbuster films like Moulin Rouge and Cold Mountain. According to Cosmopolitan, Kidman's salary peaked at $17.5 million for Bewitched in 2005.

Nicole Kidman's net worth has been widely estimated at around $250 million, but like other actors on this list, Kidman's fortune spans the currencies of the United States and her home country, Australia.

Of her wealth and marriage, Kidman has said:

"I've always been aware of privilege because both my parents came from nothing ... When we moved to America, we had nothing ... I also married a man who's totally self-made and came from a background where he said every brick in his house is a gig."

Kidman and her husband Keith Urban landed on an annual list of richest Australians in 2023, with a joint net worth of $596 million in Australian dollars, or around $388 million when converted to American dollars.
