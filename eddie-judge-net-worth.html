Eddie Judge is a Mexican television personality who has an estimated net worth of $800 thousand. Eddie Judge’s name instantly became known to public when he began dating Tamra Barney in 2012, the main cast of The Real Housewives of Orange County (RHOC).  Tamra Barney finally settled her divorce with her ex-husband Simon Barney in the fall of 2011.
