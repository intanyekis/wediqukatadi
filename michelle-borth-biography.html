Michelle Borth Bio (Wiki)

Who is Michelle Borth?

Michelle Borth is an American actress and producer who is best known for her role as Kamie in the short-lived HBO series ‘Tell Me You Love Me.

She is currently portraying the role of Catherine Rollins on the CBS crime drama ‘Hawaii Five-0’.

Michelle Borth: Age Parents, Siblings, Family

She was born on August 19, 1978, in New York City, New York, USA. She is currently 43 years old. Her parents’ names are not disclosed however, her mother is of Italian origin and owns a home improvement business.

Michelle has two young brothers and she had to take care of them as they were younger than her.

She was trained in gymnastics at Kennet Gymnastics when she was a teenager. She aspired to become a gymnast but she developed a passion for acting after she got in trouble over drugs.

Michelle Borth holds American citizenship and her ethnicity is Italian.

In which school did Michelle Borth attend?

She attended the local school and helped her mother with her household chores. She matriculated at Pace University and achieved a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theater and Art History in 2001.

Her parents were busy with their own business so she did not get enough time to spend with them. Later she joined John S. Burke Catholic High School in Goshen, New York, and graduated from there in 1996.

Michelle Borth: Professional Life, Career

Michelle Borth debuted her film career in 2002 after her college degree. She debuted in the film named ‘Apartment’ and her first TV starring was in an episode of ‘Off Centre’.

After that, she appeared as Katrina Munro in the film ‘Silent Warnings’ in 2003, Devin Sinclair in the film ‘The Sisterhood’ in 2004, and Nicole in ‘Rampage; The Hillside Strangler Murders in 2006, etc.

She made her appearance in the independent film ‘TiMER’ in 2008 with Emma Caulfield and Desmond Harrington. Likewise, she was a cast member of ABC’s ‘The Forgotten’ in 2009 and guest-starred on the TNT TV series ‘Dark Blue’ in 2010.

She portrayed the role of Steve McGarrett’s girlfriend, Lt. Catherine Rollins, a Navy Lieutenant in the 2010 version of ‘Hawaii Five-0’ and was cast as a series regular for seasons 3 and 4 but it was announced that she would not return for the fifth season.

Later on March 19, 2018, it was announced that she would return to the series as a guest star in the series for the 8th season’s twentieth episode.

Recently, Michelle Borth appeared as Elise in ‘Devious Nanny in 2018 and in the film ‘Shazam!’ in 2019 as Mary Bromfield.

The actress has not been awarded or nominated for her work to date.
