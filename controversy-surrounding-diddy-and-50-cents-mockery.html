Controversy Surrounding Diddy and 50 Cent's Mockery


Following the lawsuit filed by his ex Cassie for r@pe and abuse, 50 Cent has once again taunted fellow rapper Sean “Diddy” Combs.

On Instagram, 50 Cent captioned a selfie on Thursday, Nov. 16, with the words, “Damn brother love, brother love, brother love, you out here looking CRAZY AS A MF. LMAO.”

Combs, who changed his name to “Love” or “Brother Love” in 2017, claiming personal evolution, has been a target of 50 Cent’s criticism for some time. Just months ago, 50 Cent even publicly questioned Diddy’s sexuality.

It was no surprise to his followers when 50 Cent made mocking remarks about Diddy, 54, in response to the rape allegations.
