What does Obsidian mean?

Obsidian is a type of black or dark glass formed from cooling lava. So if you find a shiny, hard, dark piece of glass next time you’re hanging around a volcano, it’s probably a piece of obsidian. The word obsidian essentially means “stone of Obsius.” So who was Obsius? Think of an obsidian arrowhead, blade or mirror.
